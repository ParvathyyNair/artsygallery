The Solar System

The solar system is a gravitationally bound system that includes the Sun and the objects that orbit it. It's made up of:
The Sun: The star at the center of our solar system
Planets: Eight planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune
Dwarf planets: Five officially named dwarf planets: Ceres, Pluto, Haumea, Makemake, and Eris
Moons: Hundreds of moons
Asteroids: Thousands of asteroids
Comets: Thousands of comets
